Squid Game

Director: Hwang Dong-hyuk

Stars: Lee Jung-jae, Park Hae-soo, Wi Ha-jun

Desperate contestants play childhood games for a massive cash prize, but the stakes are deadly. This Korean series became a global phenomenon with its commentary on inequality and human nature. The show masterfully blends thriller elements with social criticism through compelling characters.

All of Us Are Dead

Director: Lee JQ, Kim Nam-su

Stars: Yoon Chan-young, Park Ji-hu, Cho Yi-hyun

High school students fight for survival when their school becomes ground zero for a zombie virus outbreak. This Korean zombie thriller combines teen drama with intense horror elements. The series explores friendship, sacrifice, and coming-of-age themes amid apocalyptic chaos.

Prison Break

Creator: Paul Scheuring

Stars: Wentworth Miller, Dominic Purcell, Sarah Wayne Callies

When his brother is wrongfully sentenced to death, a brilliant structural engineer deliberately gets himself imprisoned to break him out. Using hidden blueprints tattooed on his body, he orchestrates an elaborate escape plan inside Fox River State Penitentiary. The series combines intense action, strategic planning, and emotional drama as the brothers fight for freedom.
